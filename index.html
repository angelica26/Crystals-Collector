Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. Each time you click one, it will add a specific amount to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes over the amount of the random number.

You won't know the value of each crystal until you click it.

The value of each crystal changes each time a new game starts
